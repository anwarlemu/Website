CREATIVITY UNLEASHED


Start with Value

Find what you are passionate about about and follow it with all your effort.

"Passion is the drive that fuels creativity"

What do you care most about in life. Take it and work on it every waking hour.




Address Insecurities

Everyone is unique. You are never late to the party.

"Be original. You are the only you there is."

Likes and follows are quantitative measures. They do not define the quality or impact of something.

You can never please everyone. Stop trying to.




Develop Your Theme


"Find your theme. It will help as a guide if you ever start to feel derailed. It also allows people (including yourself) to better understand the mythical creature that is you."




Stay Consistent

"Don't let your inner drive die out."

Simply put, consistency is not stopping. Try to set goals that you can meet on a scheduled basis.
Set a pace that can be maintained over a long period of time. Shift your focus onto what you can control and not what is beyond it.
You need to enjoy the progress.
