Apps to Survive Online School



Anki

It’s really simple to set-up and navigate, allowing you to easily create your own flashcards or download appropriate ‘decks’ that have been made by other human beings. The app is used by over 50 million people worldwide, so you’ll have access to literally millions of sets before you’ve even made your own. The downside is that the app is costs a whopping $25 for iOS users (but that can be easily bypassed by using the website version).



Notion

Notion really changed the productivity scheme of my life. It really is a multi-functional and incredibly useful app allowing you to categorise topics, break down subjects into series of toggles and it’s probably become the all-in-one workspace for everything in my life.

It also has hundreds of pre-made templates and also thousands of user-made templates that are scattered around the internet.



Todoist

Todoist is perhaps the most popular task manager in the world. It’s used by over 20 million people and has a range of really simple but highly effective functions built into an interface that is really simple to use. Like the name suggests, you can input to-do lists, plan your day, keep track of deadlines, organise modules and generally keep track of your work all in one place.
